Application to wire

Six steps. Two weeks.

  1. [01]

    Apply

    Submit your application. We review on a rolling basis — no deadlines, no demo-day gatekeeping.

  2. [02]

    Hear Back

    Shortlisted founders get feedback within 7–10 working days. We don't leave you on read.

  3. [03]

    Pitch in Mumbai

    Meet the 100X team in person. Bring your conviction — we'll bring ours.

  4. [04]

    Term Sheet

    If it's a yes, you get a term sheet. ₹1.25 Cr on iSAFE. No valuation haggling at seed.

  5. [05]

    Diligence & Docs

    Lightweight legal and due diligence. iSAFE notes keep paperwork to pages, not binders.

  6. [06]

    Funds Wired

    Sign the iSAFE, money hits your account. Roughly two weeks from term sheet to wire.
